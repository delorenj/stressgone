Questions to Ask the Videographer
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
8 What is the earliest/latest date we can book??
9 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Videographer Specific

10 Will you be the videographer on the day of the wedding? If not, can we meet him/her/them?
11 Do you work alone or do you have assistants? If so, how many would work with you?
12 What is included in your standard package? What are your upgrades? Can we customize a package?
13 How much input do we have in regards to style and effects?
14 How many weddings have you worked?
15 Have you done an event at our venue?
16 What is your specific style? Can we see samples of your work?
17 Do you have liability insurance?
18 How long after the wedding will the video be available?
19 How many hours are included in the standard package? What is your fee for additional time?
20 What parts of the day do you capture on video?
21 Have you worked with our photographer?
22 What is your specific style? Can we see samples of your work?
23 Do you have a process for backing up the footage during the event?
24 Do you offer additional services such as photo booth or video streaming during the reception?
25 In what format is the final video delivered?
26 Do you charge for travel?
27 Can we suggest changes to the video and is there an additional cost?
28 Do you discuss background music used in the video with us?
29 Do you provide all the equipment including lighting?
