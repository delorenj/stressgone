Questions to Ask the Hotel(s)
1 Do you have rooms available on the date(s) we want?
2 What is your group rate when blocking rooms?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What payment method do you accept?
6 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
7 Do you have other blocks scheduled on our date(s)?
8 Is there a minimum or maximum number of rooms we must book for the group rate?
9 Can you block rooms for our guests that are near one another?
10 Will you accept reservations after the deadline date as long as there is availability?
11 Do you offer courtesy rooms depending on the number of guests in our party?
12 Do you offer the bride and groom a complimentary bridal suite?
13 Is it possible to include additional rooms if the agreed upon block of rooms is not enough?
14 When will the block of rooms be available to our guests?
15 Can guests extend their stay at the discounted rate to make it a long weekend?
16 Will we have a point person who will coordinate the details with us?
17 How will you let us know who has reserved a room?
18 Will there be an online code or link to use to get the group rate?
19 Is parking included in the room rate? If not, what is the additional charge? Is it negotiable?
20 Does the hotel have a restaurant?
21 Does the hotel have a bar? If so, what time does it close?
22 Do you offer "after party" packages?
23 What amenities are included? Breakfast, lunch, etc. If not, can we purchase a breakfast package?
24 If your hotel does not offer food options, can we bring in food and drinks the next morning for our guests?
25 Do you have a separate room for a breakfast buffet?
26 Is Wi-Fi included in the rate?
27 Will there be a sign in the lobby welcoming guests for our wedding?
28 Will someone at the front desk be able to direct our guests to the shuttle/limo location and time of pickup?
29 Are the rooms handicapped accessible and if so how many rooms are?
30 What are the check-in and check-out times?
31 Do you offer transportation to and from the venue and the airport?
