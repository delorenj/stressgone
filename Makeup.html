Questions to Ask the Makeup Artist
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?

Makeup Artist Specific

8 Can we book a wedding makeup trial appointment?
9 Are you able to do makeup for the bridal party as well?
10 How long will makeup take on the wedding day?
11 What size bridal party can you accommodate? How long to do make up for our bridal party of ___?
12 Are you comfortable with different ethnicities?
13 How do you handle skin allergies or severe acne?
14 How do you cover blemishes or a tattoo?
15 Do you apply false eyelashes?
16 Will a fake tan affect the makeup?
17 What beauty products do you use?
18 How will you make our wedding makeup last and look good for photos?
19 Do you have references?
20 Are you able to travel to our location in ______? Do you charge for travel?
21 Do you do hair or work with a hair stylist?
22 Do you have any specific space requirements for the trial and for the wedding day?
23 Will it be you at the wedding or another artist? What happens if you can't make it that day?
24 Do you have pictures of your work? Website or blog?
