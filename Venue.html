Questions to Ask the Venue
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 What is the earliest/latest we can book once we agree on a date?
3 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
4 What is your standard rate?
5 Do you have a contract?
6 Do you require a deposit?
7 What method of payment do you accept?
8 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?

Venue Specific

9 Are there any discounts available with regards to season, day of the week, time of day?
10 What is your minimum and maximum guest capacity? Does that include the bridal party and parents?
11 Can we make changes or substitutions to our package if there is something we do not want?
12 How many hours does the basic price include?
13 Are we able to extend the reception for an additional fee?
14 Do you have a contingency plan for unexpected events or inclement weather?
15 Do you have wedding insurance or do we need to have our own?
16 Are there bridal/groom rooms in the venue?
17 What time can we and vendors arrive at the venue the day of the event?
18 Do you have a preferred vendor list?
19 Is cake included? What are your dessert options?
20 Who will be our contact person/coordinator throughout the process and day of event?
21 Is parking/valet included or is there an additional fee?
22 What are your accommodations for guests with food restrictions or physical disabilities?
23 Is there an audio system/microphones for the band/DJ?
