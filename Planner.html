Questions to Ask the Wedding Planner/Coordinator
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 What is your standard rate?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What method of payment do you accept?
6 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?

Wedding Planner Specific

7 How many weddings will you be working on throughout our process up to our wedding day?
8 Are you a certified wedding planner? If so, from what organization?
9 Is wedding planning your full time career?
10 Can you explain the process and how we would be involved?
11 How many weddings have you planned?
12 How many staff members will be at our wedding?
13 Do you have a backup plan if you are unable to complete the planning and/or are unable to be present on our wedding day?
14 Have you worked with our venue?
15 Can you give us a general timeline?
16 Do you do destination weddings?
17 Given our vision for our wedding, what ideas do you have?
18 Will you handle all aspects of the wedding or will we be required to handle some areas?
19 Are we required to use your vendor suggestions or are we free to use vendors of our choice?
20 How do you deal with the unexpected that may come up on the day of the wedding?
21 What time will you arrive and leave on the day of the wedding?
22 Do you offer package deals or is every wedding customized?
23 How will you stay in contact? And how often?
24 Once we book with you, will you provide us with a breakdown of all the fees and how they will be allocated?
25 Will you please provide us with references?
26 What type of insurance do you carry?
27 Do you handle all payments to vendors and we reimburse you, or will we need to send separate checks to each vendor?

Wedding Coordinator Specific

28 How long have you been a wedding coordinator?
29 Will you be at our wedding and do you have additional staff that works with you?
30 Have you run into problems as a coordinator and how did you handle them ?
31 Will you be in contact with us throughout the process ?
32 Will you create a timeline for the wedding day ?
33 Will you coordinate with the other vendors?
34 Will you supply an emergency bridal kit if something goes wrong with the dresses?
