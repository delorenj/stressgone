Questions to Ask the Officiant
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?

Officiant Specific

8 Are you legally licensed to perform the wedding ceremony in our location?
9 Can you perform a ceremony that incorporates both of our religions/cultures?
10 Do you use a script or do you personalize the ceremony?
11 Can we write our own vows?
12 How long is the typical ceremony?
13 For how long have you been performing wedding ceremonies?
14 Will we have a rehearsal?
15 Do you require premarital counseling?
16 Do you complete the marriage license paperwork and file it?
17 Do you have a backup officiant if you are unable to perform the ceremony?
18 Are you able to travel to our location?
19 Will there be a travel charge?
20 Do you have any sample ceremonies that we can review?
21What type of attire will you wear?
