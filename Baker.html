Questions to Ask the Baker
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 What is your standard rate?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What method of payment do you accept?
6 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
7 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Baker Specific

8 How are your wedding cakes priced?
9 Do you have a portfolio we can view?
10 How far in advance will my cake be made?
11 Do you provide all the cake stands?
12 How many wedding cakes have you made?
13 Do you accommodate dietary needs?
14 What are our frosting, cake and filling choices?
15 Do you offer cake tastings? Is there a fee?
16 Do we choose from set designs or can we customize our cake?
17 Have you worked with our venue before?
18 What is your delivery process?
19 How long do you need to assemble the cake the day of the event?
20 Do you use fresh flowers, artificial flowers, edible flowers and do we choose the kind we want? If fresh do you coordinate with our florist?
21 Does your price include the cake topper?
22 Can we freeze the top tier? For how long? Do you provide a box for the top tier?
23 Do you charge for travel?
24 Are you a licensed baker?
25 Do we need to return any equipment and in what time frame??
