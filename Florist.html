Questions to Ask the Florist
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
8 What is the earliest/latest date we can book?
9 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Florist Specific

10 Have you worked with our venue ?
11 Will you do the arrangements yourself or do you have staff that will do it?
12 How far in advance are arrangements made?
13 Are there any recommendations to lower the cost?
14 Do you have standardized packages or is everything customized?
15 Do you have a portfolio we can look at? A website?
16 Which flowers would you recommend based on the time of year and our theme?
17 Will you duplicate arrangements from a picture we show you?
18 Are the centerpieces rentals or are they included in the price?
19 Do you supply other items such as runner, backdrop, Chuppah, votive candles, etc.? If so, what is the cost?
20 Is it possible to see a sample arrangement?
21 Will you work with our baker to decorate the cake table?
22 How do you preserve the bouquet after the wedding?
23 Is there a delivery charge?
24 What time would you set up at the venue and do you do breakdown? Is there an extra charge for this?
25 Are there any additional fees that we haven't discussed?
