Questions to Ask the Stationery/Printing Vendor
General

1 Are you available to complete all work for our date?
2 What is your standard rate?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What method of payment do you accept?
6 How long do I have to cancel for a full refund?
7 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Stationery/Printing Specific

8 How long have you been in business and have you designed many wedding invitations, save the date. thank you cards, etc.?
9 What type of printing process do you recommend for our budget and style?
10 What type fonts do you offer?
11 Do you also offer custom invitations, save the date, etc., as well as templated ones?
12 If we choose a custom invitation, is there an additional cost?
13 Can you include a picture on our save the date announcement? Is there an additional fee?
14 Does the image need to be saved in a specific format?
15 Do you also address and mail invitations? If so, what is the additional cost?
16 Once we place the order, how long before we receive the materials?
17 What is the shipping method and cost?
18 Will we be able to proof all items before final printing?
19 Do you offer an option if you are unable to complete the materials by our deadline?
