Questions to Ask the Band
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have liability insurance?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
8 What is the earliest/latest date we can book??
9 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Band Specific

10 Can you provide us with a list of your music library?
11 Where can we see you perform live?
12 How long have you been performing together?
13 Have you ever played at our venue?
14 Can you play our specialty dance songs, father/daughter dance, etc?
15 What is the deadline for specialty song requests?
16 How many hours is included in your standard package and are there any other possible charges?
17 How many breaks would you take and for how long during our event?
18 What music do you provide during breaks?
19 How much do you charge for overtime?
20 How many musicians are in the band?
21 Are any band members available to play at the cocktail hour and what is the fee?
22 What is your contingency plan in case a band member cannot be present?
23 Do you need special equipment, outlets, etc that our venue should know about?
24Do you take guest requests?
25 Can we submit a Do Not Play list?
26How do you ensure that all guests of all ages have a good time?
27 What time would you arrive at the venue to set up??
