Questions to Ask the Caterer
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
8 What is the earliest/latest date we can book??
9 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

Caterer Specific

10 Are you a licensed caterer?
11 Do you provide alcohol?
12 Do you have Liquor Liability Insurance?
13 How long have you been catering weddings?
14 Have you ever catered at our venue?
15 What is the maximum number of guests you can accommodate?
16 What is included in your basic package? What upgrades do you offer and their cost?
17 Are tips included in the package price?
18 Are all the rentals included in the package??
19 What are your food specialties?
20 Are the menus fixed or can we customize our menu?
21 Do you have food tastings and what is the cost?
22 How do you accomodate dietary restrictions?
23 How many servers/bartenders would be assigned to our number of guests?
24 What is your cancellation policy?
25 How long do you need for set-up and break-down?
