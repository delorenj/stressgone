Questions to Ask the Limo/Shuttle Provider
General

1 Are you available for our date?
2 Do you have other bookings on that day?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What method of payment do you accept?
6 How long do I have to cancel for a full refund?
7 Do you have any other bookings on our day?
8 What is your standard rate?

Limo/Shuttle Specific

9 How long have you been in business?
10 Can you provide references?
11 Are your chauffeurs/drivers/vehicles insured? What is covered?
12 Do you do background checks on your chauffeurs and drives, and are they professionally trained?
13 What is the average employment duration of your drivers?
14 What style vehicles do you have? Are they equipped with GPS?
15 Can your vehicle accommodate our size wedding party?
16 What amenities do your vehicles have? Food? Beverages?
17 How do you determine price? Time or distance? Number of vehicles?
18 Is the cost of gas included in your price?
19 Do you offer any package deals?
20 How many guest shuttles do you recommend for each hotel?
21 Do you coordinate with the hotels to inform guests of the time schedule?
22 What is the proposed shuttle schedule for pick up and drop off to and from the venue?
23 Who do we contact in case we need to speak with someone the day of the event? What number should we use?
24 Is a gratuity included in the price?
25 Do you have a backup plan in case a vehicle breaks down the day of the event?
