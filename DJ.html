Questions to Ask the DJ
General

1 Are you available on the date we want?
2 Do you have any other bookings on that day?
3 What is your standard rate?
4 Do you have a contract?
5 Do you require a deposit?
6 What method of payment do you accept?
7 What is the last date we can cancel for a full refund?
8 What is the earliest/latest date we can book??
9 Do you require full/partial payment prior to the day of the wedding?

DJ Specific

10 How long have you been a DJ for weddings?
11 How extensive is your music collection? What genres do you cover?
12 Do you offer other services such as a slide show, etc?
13 Do you offer any packages?
14 Do you act as an emcee?
15 Besides our playlist and Do Not Play list, how do you decide what to play?
16 What equipment do you use and do you supply all of it?
17 How much time do you need to set up and break down?
18 How you ever played at our venue?
19 How many breaks do you take and what is the plan for music during breaks?
20 What is your backup plan for faulty equipment during the event?
21 Do you take song requests from guests? If so, will you announce that?
22 Do you have liability insurance?
23 What is your overtime charge?
24 Are you the DJ that will do my event?
25 Can you provide references?
