Questions to Ask the Hair Stylist
General

1 Are you available to complete all work for our date?
2 What is your standard rate?
3 Do you have a contract?
4 Do you require a deposit?
5 What method of payment do you accept?
6 How long do I have to cancel for a full refund?
7 Do you have any other bookings on our day?

Hair Stylist Specific

8 Will you travel to our venue or will we need to come to your salon?
9 Do you work alone or do you have assistants?
10 How many assistants will you need to do our bridal party of ___ women?
11 Generally, how long does it take to do one hair style?
12 Do you have a backup hair stylist if you cannot make our date?
13 Do you do hair extensions and how much would they cost?
14 About how many brides have you styled?
15 Do you bring your own products (hair dryer, curling iron, etc.)?
16 Do you require payment prior to the wedding day?
17 Do you prefer one payment or can each woman pay you individually?
18 Do you do updos?
19 Do you also do makeup or do you work with a makeup artist?
20 Does your standard rate include a trial session?
21 Do you charge for travel?
22 Do you supply hair accessories?
